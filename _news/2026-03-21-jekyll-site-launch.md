---
title: GoPinas site now runs on Jekyll
date: 2026-03-21
excerpt: We can publish news, articles, and upcoming events from simple Markdown files in this repository.
---

The public site is now a [Jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com/) project compatible with [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/). Add dated items under `_news/`, long-form pieces under `_articles/`, and events under `_events/` (with `event_date` and optional `location`, `registration_url`).

Questions? See **About Us** for federation contacts.
