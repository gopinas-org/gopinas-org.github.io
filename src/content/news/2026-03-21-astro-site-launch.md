title: GoPinas site now runs on Astro
date: 2026-03-21
excerpt: We publish news, articles, and upcoming events from Markdown in this repository—no Ruby required for day-to-day edits.
---

The public site is an [Astro](https://astro.build/) static site, deployed with [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/). Add dated items under `src/content/news/`, long-form pieces under `src/content/articles/`, and events under `src/content/events/` (with `event_date` and optional `location`, `registration_url`).

Questions? See **About Us** for federation contacts.
