We had a strong turnout at our BGC meetup at **Commune Café** in Bonifacio Global City: a relaxed mix of children and adults, with newcomers picking up the basics alongside players who have been at it for years. Boards stayed busy; conversation ranged from joseki to where to eat nearby.

Thank you to everyone who joined us—first-time players and returning regulars alike.
