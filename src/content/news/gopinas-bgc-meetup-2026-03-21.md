We had a great time at our BGC meetup: a relaxed mix of kids and adults, first-timers getting their bearings alongside players who’ve been at it for years. Boards stayed busy, conversations wandered from joseki to where to eat dinner, and the room had that easy energy you get when people are genuinely enjoying the game. Medyo mainit that day, but we all had fun.

Thanks to everyone who came out—whether you were trying Go for the first time or dropping in as usual, you made the afternoon what it was.

📍 Commune Café, The Aston at Two Serendra, McKinley Parkway, BGC, Taguig
